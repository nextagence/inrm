Referral disclosure

QuickCash India is a referral/marketing landing page and not a direct lender. By clicking the application button, you may be redirected to a third-party lending partner or aggregator that manages eligibility, underwriting, KYC, pricing, loan agreement, and disbursal.

Terms transparency

Loan approval, amount, tenure, fees, APR/interest, penalties, repayment schedule, and other final terms are determined by the lender/partner. Review the Key Fact Statement and the partner’s terms before accepting any loan.

RBI materials emphasize disclosure of the annualised rate/APR, charges, and key facts for borrowers. ([rbi.org.in](https://www.rbi.org.in/commonman/english/scripts/FAQs.aspx?Id=3687&utm_source=chatgpt.com))

Risk note: Borrow responsibly. Late payment may affect your credit profile and may result in additional charges under the lender’s terms.

FAQ

Neutral answers. No fairy tales, no compliance grenade.

Am I applying directly on this website?

No. This website is a landing page that redirects you to a third-party partner for the actual application process.

Who decides approval?

The lender or partner platform decides eligibility, approval, amount, repayment schedule, and pricing.

Will the final loan amount and charges be shown before I accept?

They should be disclosed by the lender/partner before you accept the loan agreement. Review all charges, APR/interest, and repayment terms carefully. ([rbi.org.in](https://www.rbi.org.in/commonman/english/scripts/FAQs.aspx?Id=3687&utm_source=chatgpt.com))

Is this page suitable for ads?

Yes, as a pre-lander or direct CTA page. Just update the branding, support email, privacy policy, and disclosure copy before launch.
